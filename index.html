Digital Age

With the development of technology, our lives are changing day by day. Only 20 years ago, I never imagined I could take pictures and watch TV on my phone. From shopping to enjoying leisure time to portable devices, the big and small aspects of life have changed in many ways. In the past, it used to be the primary way to face anything, but now it's convenient to use with all sorts of devices.
